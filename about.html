About us

Founded by an IT professional with over a decade of corporate experience, Nestland brings the same principles of precision, transparency, and customer-first service into the world of real estate.

Having worked in industries where process, planning, and problem-solving are key, we apply a structured and professional approach to every farmhouse plot project we develop. From clear documentation to timely updates and smooth handovers, we ensure every step is transparent and hassle-free.

Our projects in Bhor and Velhe are not just about selling land - they are about creating secure investments and lifestyle opportunities for our clients. Whether you are an investor, a weekend-home dreamer, or looking for a peaceful retirement retreat, we are here to guide, support, and stand by you at every stage - even beyond the sale.
