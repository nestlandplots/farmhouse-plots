About the Project

Nature’s Grandeur at Your Doorstep, Nestland Bhor is a rare blend of lush greenery, rolling hills, and shimmering backwater views that invite you to slow down and breathe deeply. Within the project itself, you’ll find a seasonal waterfall and enjoy sweeping waterfall and hill views. The landscape is enriched by two natural blueways, dense green cover, and the region’s refreshing abundance of rainfall. Designed for comfort and leisure, the layout features multiple parking areas, a kid’s play zone, and open spaces for future amenities. Every plot is surrounded by nature’s music - flowing water, rustling leaves, and the songs of birds. Here, each season brings its own beauty, from misty monsoon mornings to golden winter sunsets. Nestland Bhor isn’t just land; it’s an experience where every day feels like an escape into the wild.
